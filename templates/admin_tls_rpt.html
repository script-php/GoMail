ℹ️ About TLS-RPT:

TLS-RPT (RFC 8460) reports are automatically generated weekly (Sundays at 00:00 UTC) and sent to remote domains' TLS-RPT addresses.

These reports help remote mail server administrators detect and fix TLS certificate or configuration issues.
