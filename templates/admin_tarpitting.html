Tarpitting introduces artificial delays on invalid SMTP commands, making spam campaigns inefficient while not affecting legitimate mail servers.

How Tarpitting Works:
  • 1st invalid: 0s delay (free pass for typos)
  • 2nd invalid: 2s delay
  • 3rd invalid: 4s delay
  • 4th invalid: 8s delay
  • 5th invalid: 16s delay
  • 6+ invalid: max delay (capped)
  • Reset: After 1 hour with no failures
