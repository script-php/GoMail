💡 How Greylisting Works:
  1. New sender (IP + sender email + recipient) is rejected with SMTP code 450
  2. Legitimate mail servers retry after a delay (the delay is {{.Domain.GreylistingDelayMins}} minutes)
  3. After delay passes, the sender is whitelisted and emails go through normally
  4. Spammy senders typically don't retry, so they never reach inboxes

Note: Whitelisted entries are kept permanently in the database. Entry cleanup removes only rejected entries older than 30 days.
